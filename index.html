History of the font

Science Gothic is a modern typeface that draws inspiration from the mid-20th century modernist design principles while infusing contemporary typographic elements. However, it's important to note that "Science Gothic" as a font name might not have an extensive historical background like some other typefaces, as it appears to be a newer creation.

Supported Languages include:

Germanic languages (which includes English, German, Swedish, and others) and the Romance languages (which includes French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese and others)
